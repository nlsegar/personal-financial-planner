# Retirement & Education Account Limits — 2025 / 2026

## 401(k) / 403(b) / 457(b)

| Limit | 2025 | 2026 |
|---|---|---|
| Employee deferral | $23,500 | $24,500 |
| Catch-up (50+) | +$7,500 | +$8,000 |
| Total employer + employee (415 limit) | $70,000 | ~$71,000 |

## IRA (Traditional & Roth)

| Limit | 2025 | 2026 |
|---|---|---|
| Contribution | $7,000 | $7,000 |
| Catch-up (50+) | +$1,000 | +$1,000 |
| Roth IRA income phaseout (MFJ) | $236K–$246K | TBD |

**Backdoor Roth**: No income limit for conversion. Contribute non-deductible Traditional IRA → convert to Roth.
Watch for pro-rata rule if any pre-tax IRA balances exist.

## HSA (Health Savings Account)

| Limit | 2025 |
|---|---|
| Individual HDHP | $4,300 |
| Family HDHP | $8,550 |
| Catch-up (55+) | +$1,000 |

Triple tax advantage: deductible contribution + tax-free growth + tax-free withdrawal for medical.

## 529 Education Savings

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Annual gift exclusion | $19,000/recipient (2025) |
| 5-year superfunding | $95,000/child lump sum |
| SECURE 2.0 rollover | Unused 529 → Roth IRA for beneficiary (up to $35K lifetime, after 15-year account age) |
